# Databricks Summit Submission Checklist

## Required Fields
- Session title selected
- Session type selected
- Track selected (Machine Learning & AI)
- Final abstract pasted
- Three learning objectives entered
- Speaker bio completed
- Speaker profile links added

## Content Quality Check
- Story-first opening in first 2-3 lines
- Clear attendee value stated
- No unverifiable performance claims
- Audience and level aligned
- Technical depth balanced with business relevance

## Packaging Check
- `SUMMIT_PROPOSAL.md` finalized
- `CFP_FORM_ANSWERS.md` finalized
- `SPEAKER_BIO_TEMPLATE.md` filled in
- Personal placeholders replaced

## Before Clicking Submit
- Read abstract out loud once for flow
- Confirm terminology matches Databricks ecosystem
- Verify no sensitive customer details are included
- Save a local copy of final submitted text
